About Yingfa
Yingfa Group, established in 1987, is a large company with 6,700 employees and an annual output value of 20 billion yuan. The group is involved in four main sectors: PV cells, PV power plants, electronics, and industrial operations. It includes 30 directly affiliated and holding companies. One of Yingfa Group's subsidiaries, Shanghai Yingfa Risen Technology Co., Ltd., owns two factories and specializes in new energy EV chargers, PV inverters, and offers OEM services. The company's experienced R&D team has 30 years of expertise in electronic control panels, solar cells, and inverters, and holds multiple invention and technology patents. Yingfa's electronic product range includes high and low voltage energy storage inverters , home photovoltaic storage and charging systems, small industrial and commercial energy storage systems, as well as AC and DC EV charging stations. These products are known for their reliable supply and high quality, giving Yingfa a competitive advantage in the market.
